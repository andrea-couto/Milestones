About Us

Flower Shop is a boutique floral studio that specializes in designs for weddings and events. The owner and his team strive to provide the best possible experience for their clients. During consultations The owner works one on one with clients to understand and design their vision for their event. Flower Shop is a unique and personal experience- how a special event should be.
